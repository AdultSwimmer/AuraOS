AURAOS QUICK START GUIDE

This guide explains how to try the AuraOS prompt framework with any large language model (such as ChatGPT).

AuraOS works by loading structured prompts into the conversation. The model reads these instructions and behaves according to the framework.

No installation is required.

---

STEP 1 — OPEN A CHAT

Open a new conversation with your preferred AI assistant.

---

STEP 2 — LOAD THE STARTUP PROMPT

Copy and paste the contents of:

STARTUP_PROMPT.txt

Send it to the AI.

This establishes the base system behavior.

---

STEP 3 — LOAD THE CORE SEED

Next, copy and paste:

Aura_Core_Seed.txt

This defines the conceptual structure of the AuraOS framework.

---

STEP 4 — LAUNCH THE SYSTEM

Paste the contents of:

Aura_Master_Launch.txt

This begins the interactive AuraOS session.

---

STEP 5 — CREATE YOUR USER HISTORY

Copy the template from:

HISTORYFORMAT.txt

Fill in your name and basic information.

Send it to the AI to initialize your personal history file.

---

STEP 6 — BEGIN USING AURAOS

You can now interact normally with the AI.

Add new entries to your history file during important conversations or projects to maintain continuity.

---

IMPORTANT NOTES

• AuraOS is a prompt framework, not executable software.
• The system works entirely within the AI's conversation context.
• Save your history file locally if you want persistent records.

---

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

• Load the files in the order listed above.
• Keep your personal history file updated.
• Use clear instructions when adding session logs.
